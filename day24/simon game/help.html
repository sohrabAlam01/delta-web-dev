1. click the start button to start the game
2. A card will be flashed let's say it card 1
3. Now you click on the card 1 to complete level 1.
4. Now another or same card will be flashed let's say it card 2.
5. Now your task is to click both the flashed cards in sequence (ex: card 1 then card 2) to comlete the level 2
6. Now another or same card will be flashed, let's say it card 3
7. Now your job is to click on all the previously flashed cards in sequence of their flashed order.
  (ex: click on card 1 then card 2 then card 3 to complete the current level)
8. one card will be flashed after each level and added it to the sequence ,
   your job is to remember all the previously flashed cards in sequence
   and click all the flashed cards in the sequence to complete the level.
